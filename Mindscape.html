MENTAL HEALTH CAMPAIGN

The Need

College and graduate students often experience anxiety and depression. Increasing awareness reduces stigma and helps students seek support, especially following the COVID-19 pandemic.

The Purpose

This campaign educates students and provides engaging, evidence-based activities that promote mental wellness and connect students to professional resources when needed.

Methods

Students developed month-long campaigns using online mental health resources, including readings, videos, and activities delivered via email, social media, and web platforms.

What’s Being Done

Materials are under clinical review and will be piloted through surveys. Future versions will target broader populations beyond students.
